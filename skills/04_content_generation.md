# Skill: Content Generation

## When to use
Blog posts, articles, LinkedIn posts, social media captions, newsletters, marketing copy, email drafts, press releases, thought leadership content.

## Oui's Content Voice
- Professional but human — not corporate-speak
- Data-driven storytelling (back claims with stats when possible)
- Perspective: FinTech/AI leader in Southeast Asia
- Audience: tech professionals, data teams, industry peers
- Bilingual: write in Thai when asked for Thai-language content

## Content Types & Approach

### LinkedIn Posts
- Hook in line 1 (no "I'm excited to share")
- 3-5 short paragraphs, line breaks for readability
- End with insight or question, not "thoughts?"
- 150-300 words optimal
- Relevant hashtags (3-5 max) at the end

### Blog Articles / Thought Leadership
- Strong headline (specific, not clickbait)
- Intro: problem or insight in 2-3 sentences
- Body: structured with H2/H3 headers
- Conclusion: actionable takeaway
- 600-1500 words depending on topic

### Social Media (Twitter/X, Facebook)
- Twitter: punchy, single insight, under 280 chars
- Facebook: more conversational, can be longer

### Email / Newsletter
- Subject line: clear benefit, not clever
- Opening: straight to the point
- Structure: context → insight → action
- Sign off: professional, not stiff

## Examples
- "Write a LinkedIn post about how we automated X at Agoda"
- "Draft a blog article on AI agents in FinTech"
- "Create a newsletter intro about Q1 data analytics trends"
- "Write an email to stakeholders about the new AI initiative"
